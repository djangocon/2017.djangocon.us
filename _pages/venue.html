Welcome to Spokane!

This year's venue is the Hotel RL by Red Lion Spokane at the Park! Located just 20 minutes from Spokane International Airport and short walk to Spokane's bustling downtown, the Hotel RL provides the perfect setting.

The Hotel RL is only steps away from the beautiful Centennial Trail, which is great for your morning run or bike ride, and from delicious cupcakes at Sweet Frostings and amazing brunch at Sante.

An Accessible Experience

We've worked with the staff at Hotel RL to accommodate the diverse needs of our attendees.

Are we missing something? Let us know

  • There is step-free access to all parts of the venue, from the parking lot to the presentation rooms.
  • Restrooms include an ADA stall with grab bars, but do not contain lowered sinks.
  • Hotel RL does not provide gender-neutral restrooms. Please use the restroom for the gender with which you identify.
  • Service animals are welcome, and there is a large park directly behind the hotel where they can be walked.
  • A quiet room will be provided. This room is not for socializing.
  • Attend a recovery meeting. Meetings at Our Club (303 W. 2nd Ave.) and Central United Methodist Church (518 W. 3rd Ave.) are each a 20-minute walk or 10-minute bus ride from the venue.
